Jerboas:

- look somewhat like miniature kangaroos



-  have long hind legs



- very short forelegs



- long tails


- tail can be longer than its head and body (true/false)


- Can have short or long ears


- are most active at twilight.


- During the heat of the day they shelter in burrows


- In summer, jerboas occupying holes plug the entrance to keep out hot air 


-  hibernate during the winter

- they cannot eat hard seeds.


- take dust baths

- mothers nurse their babies


- Jerboas are cute (true/false)
